Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 15, 2024

Introduction

VidPlays ("we", "us", or "our") operates the website vidplays.in. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you visit our site.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Usage Data: Page visits, time spent on pages, browser type, device type, and referring URLs collected through analytics services.
  • Cookies: We use essential cookies for site functionality and analytics cookies to understand traffic patterns. You can disable cookies in your browser settings.
  • Contact Information: If you use our contact form, we collect your name, email, and message content.

How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Analyze site traffic and improve content
  • Respond to contact form submissions
  • Maintain site security and prevent abuse
  • Comply with legal obligations

Third-Party Services

Our site may include:

  • Analytics: We may use Google Analytics to understand site usage. Google's privacy policy applies to data they collect.
  • Advertising: We may display ads through third-party advertising networks. These networks may use cookies to serve relevant ads. Refer to their respective privacy policies for details.

Data Retention

We do not permanently store personal data. Contact form submissions are retained for up to 90 days before deletion. Analytics data is aggregated and anonymized.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request information about data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Opt out of analytics tracking via browser settings
  • Opt out of personalized advertising

Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
